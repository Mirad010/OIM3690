# MP1 Proposal

## Client Brief

**Client:** Ali, owner of YOYO online clothing store
**Purpose:** A website to showcase and sell summer clothing online
**Audience:** People looking for summer clothes who want to see products and prices
**Key action:** Browse products and make a purchase

**Pages/sections needed:**
- Home page (brand intro, featured items)
- Shop page (photos and prices of clothes)
- Contact page (how to order/reach Ali)

**Content status:** Need product photos and prices from client

**Style preferences:** Clean, photo-focused, minimal design

**Inspiration sites:**
- Simple clothing brand websites with big product photos

## Layout Plan

- Home: big hero image with YOYO brand name, featured summer items below
- Shop: grid layout with product photos and prices
- Contact: simple form or WhatsApp link to place ordersЫ
