# Week 9 Log

## What I worked on

Started my final project, Hotel Feasibility Studio. Repo: https://github.com/Mirad010/hotel-feasibility

It's a calculator that tells you whether building a hotel at a specific location would pay for itself. My family owns property in Tashkent and we keep talking about building a hotel there, so I wanted something that actually runs the numbers instead of guessing.

Wrote PROPOSAL.md with the four required sections, set the repo up separately from oim3690 (final project needs its own repo, not inside this one or my username.github.io repo). Built the first working version: sliders for rooms, construction cost, room rate, occupancy, and fixed costs, all recalculating live. Added a break-even bar and a 15-year cash flow chart with Chart.js, plus a map (Leaflet + OpenStreetMap) and live currency conversion.
